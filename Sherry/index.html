Tax and Compliance

In order to comply with IRD requirements we will prepare a set of Annual Accounts as well as an Income Tax Statement. If your business is registered for GST we can also prepare, file with IRD and reconcile your business' GST Statements.

  • Income Tax Return Preparation for Company, Trust, Partnership
  • Income Tax Return Preparation for individual
  • Rental property tax
  • GST, FBT, PAYE, RWT registration

为了满足政府和税务局的规定，我们会帮您的公司和个人准备年终报表和年终财务报税。如果您的公司是注册GST的，我们还会帮您提供GST报表。 税务服务包括:

  • 个人，公司，信托基金报税及退税
  • 出租房税收
  • GST, FBT, PAYE, RWT, DWT
